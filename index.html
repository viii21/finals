Computer information systems are the technology-based application or solution to accomplish the business needs of an organization. Basically, the main components of computer information systems are software, hardware, databases, networks, and people. In this post, we’ll define and discuss what is computer information systems, degrees and careers in computer information systems.
CIS is a solution which helps to complete and manage a job of an organization instead of performing manually. CIS will provide you with a solution or application and you’ll put your technical skills to work and accomplish your task.
The solution or application can be software or hardware-based; software-based solutions include websites, accounting software, banking software, and online shopping software. Hardware-based solutions include CCTV, gate scanning, robotics, servers, fire alarms, and hardware firewalls.Enter text herer
